How Does Coffee Affect Health?

6 Health Benefits of Coffee:

  1. Coffee could help boost your mood!
  2. Coffee contains A LOT of antioxidants!
  3. Coffee may lower your risk of type 2 diabetes!
  4. Coffee is tied to lower rates of many other diseases!
  5. Coffee could help you become a better athlete!
  6. Coffee is NOT actually dehydrating!

5 Health Risks of Coffee:

  1. The caffeine in coffee can be VERY addictive!
  2. The caffeine can burn out your adrenal glands and over-tax your body!
  3. Consuming large amounts of coffee is not good for people with autoimmune disorders or chronic illnesses due to the large amounts of caffeine.
  4. Your sleep cycle and quality of sleep can be VERY affected!
  5. Tea may be a better option than coffee as tea has less caffeine and more health benefits.
