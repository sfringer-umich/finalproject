How Does Coffee Affect Health?

6 Health Benefits of Coffee:

  1. Coffee could help boost your mood!
  2. Coffee contains A LOT of antioxidants!
  3. Coffee may lower your risk of type 2 diabetes!
  4. Coffee is tied to lower rates of many other diseases!
  5. Coffee could help you become a better athlete!
  6. Coffee is NOT actually dehydrating!

5 Health Risks of Coffee:

  1. The caffeine in coffee can be VERY addictive!
  2. The caffeine can burn out your adrenal glands and over-tax your body!
  3. Consuming large amounts of coffee is not good for people with autoimmune disorders or chronic illnesses due to the large amounts of caffeine.
  4. Your sleep cycle and quality of sleep can be VERY affected!
  5. Tea may be a better option than coffee as tea has less caffeine and more health benefits.

Above, you have now seen the pros and the cons of drinking coffee, outlined in a video, text, and infographic format. As we can see, there are definitely more pros than cons. Although, we definitely cannot ignore or discard these cons completely, just because there are more pros. Therefore, the next question to ask is how can we get make these cons less severe and maximum our benefits from drinking coffee? To begin, one should opt for whole coffee beans and grind them just before you are about to brew your cup of joe. Additionally, black coffee is recommended if you want to experience the most benefits and eliminate the sugar and chemical overload from creamers and sugar. Next, if possible, use organic coffee beans that are pesticide-free since these are often among the most less-sprayed crops in existence. The best way to find beans that are free of chemicals are to look for ones that are 100% organic seal or by visiting a local coffee farm and buying directly from there.
