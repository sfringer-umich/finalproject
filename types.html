Coffee Drinks

Coffee is one of the most popular and common drinks worldwide. The flexibility of coffee is one of the biggest reasons that it is so popular! You can enjoy it simple and plain, sweet and chocolatey, or even bitter and tart. You can even enjoy it hot, cold, or frozen. There are so many ways to enjoy this beverage and below you can find 9 of the most common ways to order this drink today!

AMERICANO

An Americano is a shot of espresso topped with hot water. Typically this drink is prepared at a 2:1 ratio of hot water to espresso and you can order this drink either hot or iced.

FRAPPUCCINO

A Frappuccino is a trademarked beverage from Starbucks. This drink consists of either a coffee or cream base, blended with ice and other various ingredients such as flavored syrups, whipped cream, spices, and other miscellaneous toppings. A Frappuccino can often be thought of as a milkshake, as it is a very sweet, frozen beverage.

CAPPUCCINO

A Cappuccino is a strong espresso beverage made up of an equal ratio of espresso, steamed milk, and foam. A well-made Cappuccino is fairly sweet and incredibly strong in flavor. It has a bold coffee taste, from the espresso, and some sweetness from the naturally occuring sugar from the milk. If you would like the drink to be sweeter, add more sugar or flavored simple syrups such as vanilla or hazelnut!

CARAMEL MACCHIATO

A Caramel Macchiato is a very popular drink that can be served either hot or cold. A macchiato is very similar to a latte, just with less milk. This drink is typically very sweet with a combination of espresso, milk, whipped cream, vanilla-flavored syrup, and topped with a caramel drizzle! Yum!

LATTE

A Latte is one of the most popular coffee beverages consumed in the United States each day! Of the 400 million cups of coffee consumed in the US, alone, millions are variants of a Latte. A Latte is essentially created by mixing espresso with milk, traditionally a combination of steamed milk and a layer of foam. A Latte is very similar to a Cappuccino but has more steamed milk and less milk foam. This drink can be served either hot or cold and can include different flavored syrups, whipped cream, or even sugar and spice! This is a go-to drink for many coffee drinkers out there, you will not be dissapointed!

IRISH COFFEE

An Irish Coffee is a very popular coffee cocktail made of espresso, sugar, thick cream, and Irish Whiskey. This is a very sweet drink and is often preferred warm as it makes for an incredibly cozy and delicious drink!

PLAIN COFFEE

Plain Coffee is simply as the name suggests. This drink does not require any milk, sugar, or cream as it requires nothing except the brewing of coffee beans mixed with boiling hot water. While this drink is too bitter for most people, many around the world praise a strong cup of black coffee to get them through their day!

ESPRESSO

Espresso is the base for many coffee beverages consumed worldwide, such as Lattes, Macchiatos, Cappuccinos, etc. Espresso is a concentrated form of coffee served in small, strong shots and is the base for many coffee drinks. It is made from the same beans as coffee but is stronger, thicker, and higher in caffeine.

CAFE MOCHA

A Cafe Mocha is simply just a Latte with chocolate syrup added to it. Mochas tend to be very sweet, from the chocolate-flavor added to them. This drink can come hot or iced and is most popularly served topped whipped cream and chocolate drizzle.
